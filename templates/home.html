The Tulong-Dunong Program (TDP) scholarship in the Philippines is a government initiative aimed at providing financial assistance to students, specifically to those financially disadvantaged pursuing higher education. TDP Scholarhub would explore the significance of digitalization in the realm of scholarships, emphasizing the benefits, challenges, and implications of transitioning to digital platforms for scholarship application and management. It would establish a foundation for understanding the impact of digital tools on increasing accessibility, efficiency, and effectiveness in scholarship administration and shaping the future of higher education.
